    Dear Patients!
    We would like to remind you that you need to register for the OCCUPATIONAL MEDICINE in advance. A scan or a legible photo of the referral should be sent at least three days before the planned date of arrival in the attachment to the following address: reception.med@medicalClinic.com In the text of the message, please include: name, surname, PESEL number, home address, contact telephone number and an indication of the possible date of the examination. You will receive the date and time of your visit to the clinic in your return email.
    Thank you

    According to the position of the national consultant in the field of FAMILY MEDICINE of April 30, 2020. concerning the carrying out of balance tests in children during the COVID-19 pandemic, in our PHC in May, first of all, balance sheets return during the annual compulsory pre-school preparation (the so-called 6-year-old balance sheet). For the balance sheet audit:
    - must be made in advance
    - The first part of the study takes the form of teleconsultation
    - II is an examination at the clinic, arranged by registration for a specific date
    - one healthy guardian can come with a child
    - we come to the clinic at the appointed time, not earlier
